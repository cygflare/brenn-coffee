# Brenn Coffee — Brand Identity Guide

**Slow burn. Bright finish.**

---

## The mark

The Brenn logo is a typographic lockup. There is no symbol, no icon, no illustration. The brand expresses itself through three elements only: **a serif wordmark, two hairline rules, and a single ember-colored period.**

The period is the brand. It signals confidence, finality, intention. It says "we're done — that's the whole sentence."

The hairline rules give the logo its editorial, masthead-like authority. They frame the wordmark like the title page of a hardcover book.

## Color palette

| Color | Hex | RGB | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ink** | `#0a0908` | 10, 9, 8 | Primary background. The "burn." |
| **Ember** | `#E8551C` | 232, 85, 28 | Accent only. The period, the rules, the spark. |
| **Bone** | `#f5f0e6` | 245, 240, 230 | Primary text on dark. Bag interior. |
| **Bone muted** | `#a8a193` | 168, 161, 147 | Secondary text. |
| **Ink muted** | `#5a5043` | 90, 80, 67 | Secondary text on light backgrounds. |
| **White** | `#ffffff` | 255, 255, 255 | Single-color print, monochrome contexts. |

**Ember is the only accent color.** It appears as the period, the hairline rules, and select interactive elements. Never use ember as a body fill or large area. It's a spark, not a flood.

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Size guide |
|---|---|---|---|
| Wordmark | **Cormorant Garamond** (web: Google Fonts) / **Crimson Pro** (fallback) | Regular (400) | Always at least 24px equivalent |
| Descriptor / labels | **Inter** (web) / **Work Sans** (fallback) | Regular (400) | 7–11px, generous letter-spacing |

Both fonts are open-source and load freely from Google Fonts.

## Spacing rules

**Clear space:** always leave at least the height of the wordmark's cap-letter ("B") between the logo and any other element. More is better. The logo needs room to breathe.

**Minimum size:** the primary logo is legible down to 120px wide. Below that, switch to the wordmark only or the "B." icon.

**Don't:**
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change the colors of the rules or period
- Add effects (shadows, glows, gradients, outlines)
- Place on a busy photo background without darkening first
- Recreate the logo by typing — always use the supplied files

---

## Voice and copy

When the logo is paired with text, the surrounding copy should match the same restrained, editorial register:

- ✅ "Slow burn. Bright finish."
- ✅ "Single origin. Roasted to order."
- ✅ "London, since 2026."
- ❌ "Awesome coffee, super fresh!"
- ❌ "Buy our amazing beans!!!"

The brand never raises its voice. The customer comes to it.
